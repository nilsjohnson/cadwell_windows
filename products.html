The Process

In general, all products are made to spec here at the shop in Warwick, Mass. Upon completion, final fitment takes place during installation on site.

For historical reproduction, I am commited to keeping to the original design in a way that is as least invasive as possible. Maintining the value of a historic building is important to me. I always take care to work with others to make the best possible descisions when it comes to preserving history.
